Inheriting Recipe of Ah Ma

传承阿嬷好滋味

《阿嬷的秘方》创立于2020年，地点座落于柔佛州的一个小镇-昔加末。 目前公司主要经营之商业范围包括五种口味的腌制鸡扒，酱料，手工猪肉丸，粽子，五香肉卷等等。

阿嬷的秘方是属于家庭式的经营模式，为什么会取名为《阿嬷的秘方》? 这是因为所有的秘方传承于黄家的阿嬷-吕秀琴，一切的美食和产品都是由阿嬷一手制作出来。

我们知道要成为一家让人信赖和支持的品牌，除了要管理好产品的卫生品质监控到产品的口感味道都要一丝不扣的做到最好。除此之外，让在家乡外打拼的游子们能回忆起带有家乡味道的美食也是我们的宗旨之一。

为了加强我们品牌的价值，在未来的日子里，我们将会致力发展更多不同种类的产品与服务，从而满足消费者的需求，让更多不会烹饪的消费者也能煮出一道道媲美餐厅级别的美食。

"AhMa Recipe" is founded in 2020, located in a small town in Johor - Segamat. At present, the company's main business scope includes five flavors of marinated chicken chops, sauces, handmade meatballs, rice dumpling, five-spice meat rolls and so on.

AhMa Recipe is operating on family business model. The company is named after the secret recipes inherited from grandma of Ng Family - Lu Xiuqin. All the food and products are made by grandma herself.

To become a trusted and supported brand, in addition to managing the hygiene quality of the product, we must also do our best to monitor the taste of the product. Besides, it is also one of our vision to enable those who are working abroad to recall the taste of hometown.

In order to strengthen the value of our brand, we will strive to develop more products and services to meet the needs of consumers, so that our consumers are able to cook something that is on par with the restaurant outside with minimal cooking skill.
