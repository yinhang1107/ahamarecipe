Ready to serve in 3 easy steps

1

Defrost your Chicken Chop
解冻回温

Defrost your chicken chop by putting it into water without unpacking it.
无需解开包装， 放入水中解冻。

2

Start frying
煎制出锅

Pour cooking oil into hot pan. Fry each side for 5-8 minutes with medium heat until both sides are cooked thoroughly.
锅热后加入食用油，中火双面煎，各煎5-8分钟至熟透即可。

3

Ready to serve
自由享用

Serve with your favorite seasoning bag and side dishes. Enjoy your meal!
浇上调味包，搭配自己喜好的配菜，经典美味。
